About Conference

Hearty Welcome to the Virtual Conference on Artificial intelligence, Network security, Data science and iOt (NCACT 2023). It will be held offline during 25th March 2023 at Velammal Engineering College. The aim of the conference is to provide the platform for Students, Researchers, Engineers and Scientific aspirants to share their knowledge and ideas in the recent trends in the field of Artificial intelligence, Network security, Data science and IoT. The Conference focuses on providing a platform to the researchers and practitioners from both academia as well as industry to meet the share cutting-edge development in the field. The primary goal of the Conference is to promote research and developmental activities in Engineering, Technology and Management oriented towards Industry 4.0.
